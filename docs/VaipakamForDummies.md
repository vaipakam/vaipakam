# 🛡️ Vaipakam For Dummies

Welcome to **Vaipakam**! If you’ve ever wanted to borrow money using your digital collectibles as a guarantee, or earn interest by lending your spare tokens, you’re in the right place.

Think of Vaipakam as a **Digital Finance Platform**, but without a central boss. Everything is handled by code on the blockchain.

---

## 1. The Golden Rule: Your NFT is your Receipt 🎟️

In Vaipakam, whenever you start a deal (a loan or a rental), the platform gives you a special **Vaipakam NFT**.

- **Lenders:** This NFT is your "IOU." You need it to claim your money back when the borrower pays.
- **Borrowers:** This NFT is your "Claim Ticket." You need it to get your collateral (security deposit) back once you've paid off your debt.

**Warning:** If you sell or lose this NFT, you lose your right to the money or the items!

---

## 2. Ways to Use Vaipakam

### A. Lending or Borrowing Money (Tokens)

This is for when you want to borrow something like USDC (Digital Dollars) by putting up something like Ethereum (ETH) as a security deposit.

**Step-by-Step:**

1.  **The Offer:** Someone creates an offer (e.g., "I will lend $1,000 for 30 days if you give me 0.5 ETH as security").
2.  **The Deal:** You accept the offer. Your 0.5 ETH is moved into a safe digital vault (Escrow), and you get the $1,000 instantly.
3.  **Paying Back:** You pay back the $1,000 plus a little bit of interest.
4.  **The Swap:** Once paid, you use your "Claim Ticket" NFT to get your 0.5 ETH back. The lender uses their "IOU" NFT to get the $1,000 + interest.

### B. Renting Digital Items (NFTs)

Want to use a rare gaming character or digital art without buying it?

**Step-by-Step:**

1.  **Find an Item:** Look at the "NFT Rental" list.
2.  **The Deposit:** You don't put up "collateral" here. Instead, you **pre-pay** the total rent plus a 5% "safety buffer."
3.  **Usage:** You get the "User Rights" to the item. It stays in a safe vault, but you get to use it in games or apps.
4.  **Closing:** When you're done, you return the rights. You get your 5% buffer back, and the owner gets the rent money.

## 3. What if things go wrong? (Defaults & Risks) ⚠️

### "Safe" Assets (Liquid)

If you use popular tokens (like ETH or WBTC), the system tracks their price 24/7.

- **Price Drop:** If your security deposit's value drops too low, the system might automatically sell it to make sure the lender gets paid. This is called **Liquidation**.

### "Risky" Assets (Illiquid)

If you use NFTs or rare tokens as security, the system doesn't know their exact price.

- **The Default Rule:** If you don't pay your loan back on time, the **entire** security deposit is given to the lender. There is no partial refund.

---

## 4. Special Features for Pro Users

- **Early Exit:** If you are a lender and need your money back early, you can "sell" your loan NFT to someone else.
- **Refinancing:** If you are a borrower and find a better deal (lower interest), you can switch your loan to a new lender without having to pay it off first.
- **Top-ups:** If the price of your security deposit is falling, you can add more to it to prevent being "liquidated."

---

## 5. Security & ID (KYC)

To keep things legal and safe, Vaipakam uses tiers based on how much money you are moving:

- **Level 0 (Under $1,000):** No ID needed. Just connect your wallet and go.
- **Level 1 ($1,000 - $10,000):** Basic ID verification required.
- **Level 2 (Over $10,000):** Full identity check required.

## 6. Quick Tips for Success 💡

1.  **Watch the Clock:** Every loan has a "Grace Period." If you are 1 hour late on a short loan, you might lose your collateral!
2.  **Check the Mode:** The website has a **"Basic Mode"** for beginners and an **"Advanced Mode"** for experts. Start with Basic!
3.  **Anyone can pay:** A friend can pay off your loan for you, but **only you** (the holder of the Borrower NFT) can claim the collateral back.
4.  **Light or Dark:** You can switch the website theme to Light or Dark mode depending on what’s easier on your eyes.
