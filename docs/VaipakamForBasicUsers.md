# 🟢 Vaipakam User Guide: Basic Mode

Welcome to Vaipakam! This guide is for users who want a simple, safe, and guided experience. In **Basic Mode**, the website simplifies complex settings to help you lend, borrow, and rent with confidence.

## 1. What is Vaipakam?

Think of Vaipakam as a **Digital Finance Platform**.

- **Borrowing:** If you need cash (tokens like USDC) but don't want to sell your ETH or NFTs, you can use them as a "security deposit" to get a loan.
- **Lending:** If you have spare tokens, you can lend them to others to earn interest.
- **Renting:** You can "rent" the use of a special NFT (like a gaming item) for a set time without buying it.

## 2. The "Receipt NFT" Rule 🎟️

Every deal on Vaipakam gives you a special **Vaipakam NFT**.

- **Lenders:** This is your "IOU." You need it to claim your money back plus interest.
- **Borrowers:** This is your "Claim Ticket." You need it to get your security deposit back after paying the loan.
  **Warning:** If you sell or lose this NFT, you lose your rights to the deal!

## 3. How to Borrow Money (ERC-20 Loans)

1. **Find an Offer:** Browse the "Offer Book" for a deal with an interest rate and duration you like.
2. **Provide Security:** Lock your asset (e.g., 1 ETH) into a private, safe vault (Escrow).
3. **Receive Cash:** The borrowed tokens (e.g., $1,000 USDC) are sent to your wallet immediately.
4. **Repay:** Pay back the original amount + interest before the deadline.
5. **Claim:** Use your "Claim Ticket" NFT to withdraw your security deposit.

## 4. How to Rent an NFT

1. **Pick an Item:** Find a character or item in the NFT Rental list.
2. **Pre-pay:** You pay the rent upfront plus a small 5% safety buffer.
3. **Use it:** You get "User Rights" to use the item in games or apps. The NFT stays in a safe vault.
4. **Done:** When the time ends, your rights expire, and your 5% buffer is returned.

## 5. Safe (Liquid) vs. Risky (Illiquid) Assets

- **Safe Assets:** Popular tokens like ETH or USDC. If their price drops too much, the system might sell a small piece to protect the lender (Liquidation).
- **Risky Assets:** NFTs or rare tokens. **Warning:** If you miss your deadline, the **entire** security deposit is given to the lender.

## 6. Security & ID (KYC)

To stay safe and legal, we use tiers:

- **Under $1,000:** No ID needed.
- **$1,000 - $10,000:** Basic ID check required.
- **Over $10,000:** Full ID check required.
