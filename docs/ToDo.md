Receiver mints VPFI on Base, OFT-sends VPFI back to buyer, replies `BUY_SUCCESS`. **Only on `BUY_SUCCESS` arrival at the source-chain adapter is `_releaseToTreasury(amt)` called** — that's when ETH actually moves to treasury. So today the order is opposite of what T-031's premise asks: VPFI mints first, treasury inflow is eventually-consistent across the LZ round-trip.

  _Threat model:_ a forged inbound `BUY_REQUEST` on the receiver mints VPFI on Base **without any corresponding source-chain ETH inflow** — there's nothing in escrow on any source chain when the message is forged. Damage today is bounded by: (1) DVN forge cost — must collude with 3 DVN operators per CLAUDE.md cross-chain policy (LZ Labs + Google Cloud + Polyhedra/Nethermind, plus 1-of-2 from BWare Labs / Stargate-Horizen — operator diversity is load-bearing); (2) receiver caps `VPFI_BUY_GLOBAL_CAP` + `VPFI_BUY_PER_WALLET_CAP` on the Diamond; (3) adapter caps `setRateLimits(50_000e18, 500_000e18)` per source chain; (4) `pause()` lever on both contracts (precedent: April 2026 cross-chain incident's 46-min pause blocked ~$200M of follow-up drain).

  _To flip the order (VPFI mint only after source-chain ETH proven in treasury) the receiver must independently verify a source-chain state — that's a cross-chain proof, not just an LZ message._ Three real options: (A) **zkLightClient / Hashi state proof** — receiver verifies Merkle proof that "BuyAdapter on chain Z received Y ETH from wallet X". Cryptographically sound. Cost: ~100K–500K gas per buy + seconds-to-minutes proof latency + new dependency. (B) **Optimistic + 24h challenge window** — record intent on `BUY_REQUEST`, mint after 24h if no fraud claim filed. Adds 24h to every buy — kills retail UX. (C) **Status quo + tight caps + monitoring.** Even with options A/B you still need the LZ message (or a state proof) to know **which buyer wallet** paid — same trust class for that field.

  _Recommended near-term action_ (cheap, no contract changes, big blast-radius reduction): build a **cross-chain reconciliation watchdog Worker** alongside the hf-watcher. Listens for `BridgedBuyProcessed` on Base, looks up the matching `BuyRequested` on the named `originEid`'s chain via its public RPC, alerts + auto-`pause()` on mismatch through a low-quorum operator multisig. ~1-min cadence, ~1-hour worst-case detection lag, runs on the same Cloudflare lane as hf-watcher. Doesn't prevent the forge; bounds post-detection blast radius the same way the April 2026 incident's reactive pause did.

  _Decision/next step:_ defer Options A and B until cumulative bridged volume exceeds ~$10M; ship the watchdog Worker before opening mainnet to volume above the current `setRateLimits(50_000e18, 500_000e18)` per-source-chain caps. Re-open T-031 when (a) cumulative bridged volume crosses $10M, OR (b) a credible LZ DVN advisory lands, OR (c) we widen the per-source-chain caps. Treat this entry as the audit trail.
